East 8 Hold Up

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 oz Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Aperol
  • 0.75 oz Pineapple juice
  • 0.5 oz Lime juice
  • 1/2 oz Sugar syrup
  • 1 tsp Passionfruit syrup
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

1. Add vodka, Aperol, pineapple juice, lime juice, Passionfruit syrup, and sugar syrup to a shaker.
2. Fill the shaker with ice cubes and shake well for 15 seconds.
3. Double strain the mixture into a chilled glass.
4. Garnish with a thin slice of pineapple or a twist of orange peel or Passionfruit.
5. Serve and enjoy your East 8 Hold Up!
