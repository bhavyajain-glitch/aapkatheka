Basil Smash

"The Basil Smash is a delightful blend of fresh basil, crisp gin, zesty lemon, and a hint of sweetness. Known for its vibrant green hue and refreshing flavor, this cocktail is perfect for any occasion. Shake, sip, and savor the taste of herbaceous elegance!"

East 8 Hold Up

"The East 8 Hold Up is a tropical-inspired cocktail that combines the exotic sweetness of pineapple with the tangy brightness of passion fruit and lime. Paired with the crispness of vodka and the aromatic touch of Aperol, this vibrant drink is a playful and modern twist on classic tropical flavors. Perfect for summer evenings or when you’re dreaming of sunny shores!"

Boulevardier

"The Boulevardier is a timeless classic that blends the bold richness of bourbon with the bittersweet harmony of Campari and sweet vermouth. With its warm, velvety texture and complex flavor profile, this cocktail is perfect for those who enjoy a sophisticated and robust drink. A refined cousin of the Negroni, the Boulevardier exudes elegance and charm in every sip."

Savoia Americano

"Savoia Americano is an elegant aperitif that combines the rich heritage of Italian craftsmanship with modern sophistication. With its harmonious blend of red wine, bitter botanicals, and sweet vermouth, it offers a perfectly balanced flavor profile that’s both refreshing and complex. Whether enjoyed on its own over ice or as a base for cocktails, Savoia Americano embodies timeless Italian charm in every sip."

Negroni

"The Negroni is a bold and sophisticated classic that balances the bittersweet charm of Campari with the depth of gin and the rich sweetness of vermouth. Known for its vibrant red hue and complex flavor profile, this Italian aperitif is a symbol of elegance and timeless taste. Perfect as a pre-dinner drink, the Negroni offers a harmonious blend of bitter, sweet, and botanical notes in every sip."

Purple Reign

"The Purple Reign is a visually stunning and luxurious cocktail that combines vibrant fruit flavors with a touch of floral elegance. Its mesmerizing purple hue, achieved with ingredients like blackberries or violet liqueur, makes it as eye-catching as it is delicious. Sweet, tangy, and slightly aromatic, the Purple Reign is a royal treat perfect for special occasions or whenever you want to feel a little extra."
