Purple Reign

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Gin
  • 1/2 oz Crème de Violette
  • 1/2 oz Lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz Grenadine syrup
  • Ice cubes
  • Edible flower for garnish

Instructions:

1. Fill a shaker with ice cubes.
2. Add vodka, crème de violette, lemon juice, and simple syrup.
3. Shake well until chilled.
4. Strain the mixture into a chilled coupe glass.
5. Garnish with an edible flower and enjoy your Purple Reign!
